About Niass GitDataLab

Niass GitDataLab is my personal website dedicated to Data Science. It showcases my projects completed during my Master's degree, co-accredited by the University of Lille, Centrale Lille, and IMT Nord. Here you can discover my work in data analysis, visualization, and machine learning.

This website highlights my GitHub projects, which I developed and customized to illustrate my skills and background in data science.

The visuals and graphics used on the site were created by me to accompany my analyses and demonstrations.

Niass GitDataLab uses HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, allowing for smooth navigation and easy content customization.
